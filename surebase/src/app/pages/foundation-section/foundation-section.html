What is Surebase?

Your Foundation for Modern Insurance.

Surebase is the B2B insurance aggregation platform built to back the entire insurance stack.

We consolidate thousands of insurance products into a single, unified API, enabling seamless, secure connections between licensed insurance providers and distribution partners like brokers, fintechs, banks, and more.
